Bestomak Canyon

One of the canyons of the Charyn National Park. The length of the canyon is 3 km . The canyon stands out for its variety of colors and depth.

Chunja Hot Springs

Thermal springs 300 km from Almaty with an average water temperature of 38 degrees. The water in the springs is considered therapeutic.

Big Almaty Lake

A high-altitude lake at an altitude of 2500m above sea level surrounded by glaciers and high peaks. The most popular attraction of Almaty.

The plateau of Assi and Turgen

Lake

Issyk Lake

A mysterious lake 60 km from Almaty, emerald-colored. One of the most popular places for day trips.

Place

Altyn Emel National Park

The pride of the South of the Dzungarian Alatau and the largest national park in Kazakhstan. Altyn Emel National Park is located 250 km from the city of Almaty.

Lake

Kaindy Lake

The youngest alpine lake in Kazakhstan and the most popular attraction. In 1911, after an earthquake, water flooded the forest and formed this miracle of nature.
